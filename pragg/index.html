About us

Pragma Technology Co., Ltd. is a system integrator. Established in June 2003 by a group of expert engineers specialized in Information and Communication Technology field (ICT). Its goal is to provide consulting, designing and implementing total IT solutions. Based on usage and budget, a company also aims to fulfill customer’s requirements with various technologies.
Missions
Pragma Technology Co., Ltd. commits to provide the best Information and Communication Technology to increase the customer’s business efficiency:
1. Pragma Technology Co., Ltd. focuses on providing consulting and implementing IT system, considering the most authentic use according to customer’s requirement, budget, and the future expansion capacity. These are done by professional engineers for the benefit and satisfaction of customers..
2. Pragma Technology Co., Ltd. strongly attempts to develop continuous service due to the fast changing technology
